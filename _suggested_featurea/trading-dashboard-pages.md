# Trading Bot App — Suggested Additional Pages

## 🧠 1. Trades Page
- Full trade history (open + closed)
- Filters (asset, date, win/loss)
- Columns: symbol, entry, exit, pnl, duration, strategy

---

## 📊 2. Analytics Page
- Equity curve
- Drawdown chart
- Win rate
- Profit factor
- Avg win/loss
- Trade frequency

---

## ⚠️ 3. Risk Page
- Exposure %
- Open risk $
- Daily loss vs limit
- Max drawdown
- Risk per trade

---

## 📡 4. Signals Page
- Generated signals
- Approved vs rejected
- Reasons for rejection

---

## 🧾 5. Activity / Logs Page
- Full logs
- Filter by type (error, trade, cycle)
- Timestamped entries

---

## 🤖 6. Agent Control Page
- Start / Stop bot
- Toggle crypto/stocks
- Adjust risk %
- Force run cycle

---

## ⏱️ 7. Scheduler / Health Page
- Last run time
- Next run estimate
- Run duration
- Missed cycles
- Failed runs

---

## 🧠 8. Strategy Config Page
- Timeframe
- Indicators on/off
- Thresholds (RSI, EMA)
- Max positions

---

## 📈 9. Portfolio Page
- Total equity
- Asset allocation
- Crypto vs stocks %

---

## 🚨 10. Alerts Page
- Configure alerts
- Trade alerts
- Risk alerts
- Error alerts

---

## 🧪 11. Backtesting Page
- Run backtests
- Compare strategies
- Visualize results

---

## 🧭 12. Market Overview Page
- Market open/closed
- Volatility
- Trending assets

---
