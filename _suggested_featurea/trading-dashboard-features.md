# Trading Bot Dashboard — Suggested Features

## 🔥 1. Strategy Performance Panel
- Win rate (%)
- Avg profit per trade
- Avg loss per trade
- Risk/reward ratio
- Profit factor

---

## 📊 2. Equity Curve
- Equity over time chart
- Drawdown overlay

---

## ⚠️ 3. Risk Monitor
- Current exposure (%)
- Daily loss vs max allowed
- Open risk
- Active trades count

---

## 📡 4. Signal Pipeline Breakdown
- Symbols scanned
- Signals generated
- Signals approved
- Trades placed

---

## 🧠 5. Per-Asset Performance
- Stocks vs Crypto breakdown
- Trades count
- PnL per asset class

---

## 🧾 6. Trade History Table
- Symbol
- Entry / Exit
- PnL
- Duration
- Strategy reason

---

## 🧠 7. Decision Reasoning
- Why trades were approved/rejected
- Indicators used

---

## 🚨 8. Alerts / Notifications
- Trade executed
- Daily loss hit
- Bot errors
- Missed cycles

---

## ⏱️ 9. Scheduler Monitor
- Last run duration
- Avg cycle time
- Missed runs
- Failed runs

---

## 🧪 10. Backtest vs Live Comparison
- Backtest PnL
- Live PnL
- Deviation

---

## 🧩 11. Strategy Controls
- Toggle crypto/stocks
- Adjust risk %
- Pause bot

---

## 📉 12. Drawdown Tracker
- Current drawdown
- Max drawdown

---

## 🧭 13. Market State Indicator
- Overlap active/inactive
- Volatility level
- Trend state

---

## 🧱 14. Error Monitoring Panel
- Last error
- Error count
